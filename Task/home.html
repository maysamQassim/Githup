Bank of Iraq

promoting the good of the people of the Iraq by maintaining monetary and financial stability.

The Central Bank of Iraq has been established since 1947.

The Bank of Iraq prudentially regulates and supervises financial services firms through the Prudential Regulation Authority.

Banking Law No. 94 of 2004

The Banking Act was enacted on September 19, 2004. The law guarantees the legal framework for a banking system in line with international standards and seeks to enhance confidence in the banking system.
